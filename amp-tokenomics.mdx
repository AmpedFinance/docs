# AMP Tokenomics

The AMP token is the native utility and governance token of the Amped Finance ecosystem. This page provides an overview of the token distribution and vesting schedule.

## Distribution Explanation

- **TGE (Token Generation Event)**: The percentage and amount of tokens that become available at the Token Generation Event.
- **Cliff**: The period (in months) during which tokens remain locked after TGE before vesting begins.
- **Vesting Period**: The timeframe over which tokens become gradually available after the cliff period ends.
- **First/Last Tokens**: Indicates the month (from TGE) when the first and last tokens become available for the respective category.

## Category Breakdown

- **Protocol Incentives (28%)**: The largest allocation, dedicated to incentivizing protocol usage and growth.
- **Liquidity (15%)**: Allocated to ensure liquidity across various trading pairs and markets.
- **Reserve (11%)**: Maintained for future ecosystem development and unforeseen needs.
- **LightLink Foundation (10%)**: Allocated to the LightLink Foundation for strategic partnerships and development.
- **Team (9%)**: Allocated to the team members, with appropriate vesting to ensure long-term alignment.
- **Public (8%)**: Tokens available for public sale and distribution.
- **Private (7%)**: Allocated to private investors with vesting schedules.
- **KOL & Rewards (10%)**: Combined allocation for Key Opinion Leaders and various reward programs.
- **Advisors (2%)**: Allocated to project advisors with extended vesting.
