## How It Works

1. Price feed contracts provide real-time price data for each supported asset through the `latestAnswer` function

2. Amped Finance's Vault PriceFeed contract retrieves the price from the respective price feed contract using the `latestAnswer` call

## Price Updates

Thanks to the efficiency of our supported networks, we can:
- Frequently refresh prices
- Keep price data up to date
- Maintain accuracy with minimal gas costs
