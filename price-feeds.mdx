## How It Works

1. Bridge contracts use the `latestAnswer` function to fetch prices from the [Pyth price feed contract](https://phoenix.lightlink.io/address/0xA2aa501b19aff244D90cc15a4Cf739D2725B5729)

2. Amped Finance's Vault PriceFeed contract then retrieves the price from the bridge contract using the `latestAnswer` call

## Price Updates

Thanks to LightLink's gasless Enterprise Mode, we can:
- Frequently refresh prices
- Keep price data up to date
- Maintain accuracy without incurring gas fees
