# AMP Token

AMP is the governance token of Amped Finance, designed to align the interests of traders, liquidity providers, and platform stakeholders.

## Token Utility

AMP tokens serve multiple purposes in the ecosystem:

1. **Governance**
   - Vote on protocol changes
   - Participate in treasury management
   - Influence platform parameters

2. **Staking Rewards**
   - Earn a share of platform fees
   - Receive esAMP rewards
   - Accumulate Multiplier Points

3. **Platform Access**
   - Special trading features
   - Enhanced rewards
   - Priority access to new features
